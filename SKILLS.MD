# B2B Lead Qualification & Outreach Skill

## Description
This skill automates the identification, research, and personalized copywriting for high-value B2B business leads from an ingested data file.

## Capabilities
- **Lead Filtering:** Applies deterministic firmographic scoring gates using industry sector and employee size.
- **Corporate Research:** Connects to Gemini to synthesize mid-market corporate priorities.
- **Outbound Copywriting:** Drafts conversion-oriented, 3-sentence personalized cold email outreach.

## Execution Trigger
`python main.py`
